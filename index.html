About Us

Shri Anandpur Trust, established in 1954, is located at P.O.Shri Anandpur near Esagarh, Distt. Ashoknagar (MP), is an embodiment of spirituality, social services, truth and love and it adorns the State of Madhya Pradesh by its precious higher ideals taught by our Rishis of ancient past. All these activities are in furtherance of the mission of Shri Paramhans Advait Mat for bestowing peace, love and joy in the lives of suffering humanity through thousands of Sadhus, Sanyasis and selfless workers
